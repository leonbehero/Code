A Strategic Vision for Quality Assurance by Mingtao Zhang

Vision & Objective

To measure quality at scale and drive continuous improvements in our people, processes, products, and tools. This strategy aims to enhance business performance and customer experience by acting as a catalyst for proactive optimization, innovation, and a customer-centric culture.
